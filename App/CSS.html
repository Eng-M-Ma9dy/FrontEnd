- in this case i have three child, every child take width 30% from parent, and i have free spase 10% of parent, and i have two child take grow 2, and one child take grow 1

- in this case i have total 5 grow of all child, the grow will Divide the 10% of parent on 5=2% / child1 30% + (2% x 2) / child2 30% + (2% x 1) / child3 30% + (2% x 2)

- Default Value of Grow=0

*/
